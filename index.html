About 02 — Profile

I'm Rich Palmer, a graphic and digital designer based in Nottingham. Working in the creative industry for over 35 years. I have experience working for a huge variety of clients across many business sectors including sport, fashion, retail, hospitality, security, construction, and more.

My approach has remained the same throughout my design career: remain true to the principles and values of 'good' design but embrace new technology and bend the rules, test the principles & create some chaos.

Currently working in digital design, bringing clarity and purpose to brand systems & defining brand VI and toolkits. Specifically working within the sport, outdoor and fashion markets.
